Lesson One

The Art Of The Pitch

The First Step Of Making A Story

Welcome to Lesson One of CitrusTV News training! In this episode, we break down everything you need to know about finding, developing, and pitching strong story ideas as a reporter.

From understanding what a pitch is to navigating the CitrusTV pitch document and Sunday meetings, this guide walks you through the full process step by step. Learn how to discover story ideas by engaging with your community, staying active on local platforms, and tapping into newsroom resources. We also cover how to research effectively, find compelling sources, and plan stories that are not only informative—but visually engaging for a broadcast audience.

You’ll get practical advice on what makes a pitch stand out, how to think about feasibility and timing, and why having backup ideas is essential. We also highlight common mistakes to avoid, like conflicts of interest, unrealistic expectations, and duplicate coverage.

Finally, we show you how to present your pitch clearly and concisely to your news director, and what to expect during pitch meetings.

Stick around to hear insights from CitrusTV’s 2025 News Director on what makes a pitch truly successful.

🎥 Whether you're new to reporting or looking to sharpen your skills, this lesson sets the foundation for creating impactful stories at CitrusTV News.
